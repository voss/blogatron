Who are you?


I am Jonas, a regular Joe, breathing oxygen since the Vintage year 1975. Living, studying, and working [in that order] in Copenhagen -- the City of [insert tag-line here], located in the proud [at least before the last election] Kingdom of Denmark, home of Hamlet, H.C.Andersen and Jens, the not so famous guy living a couple of blocks from me.

Do you do anything for a living?


I work as a tech-supporter for staff and students at the Institute of Geography at the University of Copenhagen.

Do you study or do you just gawk at your screen all day?


I do gawk a lot, but besides that, I just finished my Bachelor of Science in Developmental Geography. Only took me 5 years and a plethora of exams, and my bachelor of science thesis is available here. My new adventure begins in September at the IT-University of Copenhagen, where I'll be studying Design, communication, and media.

Why this blog?


I am supposed to fill this blog with anecdotes from my traveling -- more specifically from the one year trip I made aged 19-20. Admittedly they are few and far apart so far [one here, here, and one here], but I'll work on it. The easy way to see if I've added any travel stories lately will be to check the right column of the archives section. Underneath 'Travel anecdotes' will be listed the most recently added in that category. In between travel stories I'll fill up the lines on this site with notes of insignificance of my doings and not doings.

What's up with the name, it's not that hard to spell venture is it?


The name verture is a mix of the two words verge and future, meaning "always on the brink of things to happen". I constructed it in high school where I did som [mighty poor] graphic work for a thesis of a friend of mine using Aldus Pagemaker on my PB 145b. Later on, I came to think of it as the overture without the o. Overture meaning a first step toward something else, something different, or the introduction to something bigger as in an opera or a ballet.

Now it is made up of both of these meanings, and I find that it encompasses a lot of good imagery of me being a person on the move, both inside and outside.

blogging at 2k means...well, that I am blogging, and that it is done at 2000 -- my postal district. 2k rather than 2000 to catch that millenium feel of course. Hopefully I won't be moving anytime soon, and if I do, it would have to be to another x000 district -- blogging @ 2670 just doesn't sound as hip.

When constructing this site, what weapons do you wield?


OS X, BBEdit 6.5.2, PS7, Illustrator 10, NetFinder 2.3.1, Moveable Type v.2.5, PHP, XHTML, CSS, and various browsers. Sometimes brain is included.
